MLR Career Stats

Welcome to the MLR Career Stats page!

Here you can find career stats for hitters and pitchers in MLR.

You can also view customized leaderboards for hitting and pitching.

Some of the stuff will be slightly different from the stats sheet. For some I don't know why. This might be slightly inaccurate. If you find anything blatantly incorrect feel free to ping me: pull#0053

By default, certain stats ignore IBBs, Auto BBs, and Auto Ks. If you're viewing the custom leaderboards page, however, you can include these by selecting the options with the suffix _2. For example, OBP ignores IBBs and Auto BBs while OBP_2 includes these.

Also Runs Scored for hitters might be slightly inaccurate due to pinch runners. It's hard to track pinch runners using the PA logs.

Season 1 data in general might be slightly incorrect... I've found many discrepancies with the official stats pages unfortunately.

These also don't automatically update. Last updated: 7/8/2020

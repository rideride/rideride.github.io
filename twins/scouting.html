Twins Scouting

Welcome to the Minnesota Twins Scouting page!
Here you can find scouting on pitchers, as well as hitters. You can even use this page to scout yourself, as a hitter or pitcher!
Please visit the "Pitcher Scouting" or "Hitter Scouting" tabs above to view scouting.
This site is a modification of the Redditball site, which uses the same databases to draw data and form predictions for next pitches based on past pitch data, differences between pitches, difference from previous swing, jump frequency, results after outcomes, park factors, "meme" frequency, and more.
Please do not share this site with anyone outside the Minnesota Twins / Kerbin Gemini. Thanks, and go Twins!
